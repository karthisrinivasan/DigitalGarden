# Fitzhugh Nagumo Model of Neurons
- $v$ - membrane voltage
- $w$ - recovery variable
- $\frac{dv}{dt}=v-\frac{1}{3}v^3-w+I$
- $\frac{dw}{dt}=\alpha V-\beta W$
